Read with Your Child

The Little Star

Story: Once upon a time, there was a little star who felt too small to shine brightly. But one night, when all the other stars were hidden by clouds, the little star shone its light and guided a lost bird home. The bird thanked the little star, and from then on, the little star knew that even small lights can make a big difference.

Moral: Everyone has something special to offer, no matter how small they feel.

Discussion Questions

  • What did the little star do to help the bird?
  • How did the little star feel at the beginning of the story?
  • What can you do to help others, even if you're small?
